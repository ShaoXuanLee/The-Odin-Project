Spicy Tuna Sushi

Ingredients

  • Spicy Tuna
  • Cucumber
  • Seaweed
  • Rice
  • Crab Meat
  1. Spread the rice on the seaweed
  2. Flip the seaweed
  3. Slice the avocado
  4. split crab meat into half
  5. Put the cucumber, crab meat and avocado on the seaweed
  6. Roll it
  7. Use maki to shape
  8. Cut it into 8 pieces
